# Sustainability Goals — One & Only Cape Town / Kerzner International

## Corporate Framework
**Parent Company:** Kerzner International  
**Overarching Commitment:** Net Positive Hospitality (World Sustainable Hospitality Alliance, joined April 2025)

---

## Active Sustainability Commitments

### 1. EarthCheck Certification (Mandatory since 2019)
- Every Kerzner property must maintain EarthCheck certification
- Annual benchmarking, third-party audited
- Continuous improvement in energy, water, carbon, and waste KPIs
- Reference: One & Only Reethi Rah achieved **Platinum** in 2025 — portfolio benchmark

### 2. Net Positive Hospitality (WSHA — 2025 commitment)
- Framework: Give back more than consumed across Planet, People, Place, Prosperity
- Aligned with Paris Agreement and UN SDGs
- Requires measurable progress on resource intensity metrics year-on-year

### 3. Carbon Direction
- No published portfolio-wide net-zero date (as of 2026)
- Emirates One&Only Wolgan Valley: carbon neutral for 9+ consecutive years — blueprint for portfolio
- UAE-aligned operations targeting net-zero by 2050
- South Africa operations: carbon tax compliance + reduction roadmap

### 4. Water Stewardship
- Cape Town Day Zero (2018) shaped the institutional culture — water efficiency is a priority
- Target: Mediterranean EarthCheck Excellent band (<0.60 m³/guest night)
- Leak detection and base load monitoring are operational priorities

### 5. Energy Reduction
- Year-on-year reduction in absolute energy consumption
- ToU optimisation to reduce peak electricity costs
- Investigation of renewable energy integration (solar PV, green tariffs)
