Triggers

The operational automation layer. 342+ alert rules across the entire organisation, configurable by parameter, condition, threshold, and duration. Unlike every other report, Triggers operates at the organisation level — not point-specific — providing a unified control surface for all monitoring alerts.

1. Capability & Purpose

Triggers transform Augos from a retrospective analysis platform into a proactive monitoring system. Instead of discovering problems during the next morning's dashboard review, triggers push alerts in real-time when thresholds are breached.

  • Organisation-Wide Scope: ⚠️ This is the only report that spans the entire organisation — not filtered by point ID. All 342+ triggers across all sites are visible and manageable from one screen.
  • CRUD Operations: Create, read, update, delete triggers directly from the interface.
  • Duration-Based Thresholds: Triggers can fire on instantaneous breach or sustained breach (1 min, 5 min, 15 min, up to 10,080 min / 7 days).
  • Card-Style Rows: Each trigger is displayed as a card showing the parameter, condition, threshold, duration, and site.
