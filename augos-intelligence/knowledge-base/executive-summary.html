Manufacturing Module Report Definition Version 2.1

Executive Summary

The macroeconomic command console for plant leadership. It synthesizes millions of millisecond-level machine events into a universal OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) percentage, providing an immediate, verifiable read on plant health and production velocity.

1. Capability & Purpose

Manufacturing leadership often suffers from data latency. Morning continuous improvement (CI) meetings are typically characterized by debating the accuracy of shift reports rather than resolving the actual constraints. The Executive Summary replaces this debate with an indisputable, mathematically derived truth.

  • Single Source of Truth: Aggregates raw telemetry, product definitions, and operator inputs into one holistic KPI (OEE).
  • Immediate Triage: Decomposes the OEE score into its triad—Availability, Performance, and Quality—instantly directing leadership to the core operating constraint.
  • Temporal Context: Renders a visual "Day in the Life" production timeline, exposing the cadence of planned stops alongside unexpected failures.
The Augos Advantage

Unlike siloed SCADA dashboards, the Executive Summary unifies Equipment state with Product context (which SKU was running?) and Human attribution (who was operating?). It doesn't just display that a line was down; it calculates the exact percentage of theoretical capacity that was lost, and immediately categorizes the loss vector.

2. Operational Mechanics (The OEE Triad)

The Executive Summary relies on the classic OEE formulation, but mathematically hard-links each component to direct PLC logic to remove human estimation bias.

Metric Pillar Definition Formula What it actually measures
Availability Operating Time / Planned Production Time When the machine was scheduled to run, was it actually running? It isolates the impact of Unplanned Stops (breakdowns, downstream blockages, material starvation).
Performance (Total Units / Operating Time) / Ideal Run Rate When the machine was running, was it running at its theoretical maximum speed? It isolates Speed Loss and Micro-stops.
Quality Good Count / Total Count Of the products produced, how many passed specification? It isolates Scrap, Rework, and Yield loss. (Note: Often ingested from external QA systems).

By multiplying these three percentages together (Availability × Performance × Quality), the report yields the definitive OEE %. A world-class OEE benchmark is typically considered ≥85%.

4. Strategic Opportunities Exposed

The Executive Summary is a navigational tool. It shouldn't be used for root-cause analysis (that is the domain of the Downtime Report), but rather to point leadership in the correct direction for that analysis.

Diagnostic Profile Next Step / Navigation Management Action
Low Availability + High Performance Navigate to Downtime Report The machine runs fast but breaks often. Focus maintenance response times and investigate chronic component failure.
Low Performance + High Availability Navigate to Operator Analysis or Job Card The machine runs constantly but slowly. Investigate "fear of failure" slow-running by specific crews, or sub-standard raw material causing minor jams.
OEE Drops on Specific Shifts Navigate to Shift Report Investigate cross-shift knowledge transfer and training deficits. Are changeovers taking twice as long at night?

5. Data Architecture & Integration
