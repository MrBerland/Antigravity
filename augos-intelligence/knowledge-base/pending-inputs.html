Manufacturing Module Report Definition Version 2.1

Pending Inputs Ledger

The action-oriented "To-Do List" for operators and shift supervisors. It collects every production delay or anomalous event detected by the PLC that requires human context (a reason code) to finalize the OEE calculation.

1. The Hybrid Data Model: Machine Meets Human

Augos automatically detects when a machine stops. A photocell drops low, a motor faults—the platform registers an "Unplanned Delay" down to the millisecond. However, the machine rarely knows why. Was it a mechanical failure, or was the operator simply on a bathroom break during a scheduled run?

  • The Gap: The PLC logs the state change. The Operator must provide the context.
  • The Ledger: Rather than forcing operators to stare at a screen waiting to input codes, the Pending Inputs ledger aggregates all unclassified events into a queue. Operators can classify five minor stops at once when they have a free moment.
Micro-Stop Exemption

Operators are not asked to code a 4-second jam 300 times a shift. Augos configuration naturally groups and auto-classifies sub-threshold events as "Minor Stops" inside the OEE Performance pillar. Only stops exceeding a predefined duration (e.g., 2 minutes) trigger a required input in the ledger.

2. Tagging Compliance & Data Integrity

A plant's intelligence is only as strong as its root-cause data. If 40% of all downtime is tagged "Unknown" or "General Stop," the CI team cannot act. The Pending Inputs ledger enforces data hygiene.

Operational Mechanism Business Impact
Shift Lockout Incoming shifts cannot classification-clear events that occurred before they clocked in. The outgoing supervisor must clear their queue before handover is accepted.
Mandated Hierarchy Operators must traverse the strict 3-level Reason Tree (Level 1 Category → Level 2 Sub-Cat → Level 3 Reason). They cannot bypass it with a generic text entry.
Commentary Enforcement For extremely long stops (e.g., > 1 hour), the system can require textual commentary alongside the categorical code to aid maintenance engineers.
