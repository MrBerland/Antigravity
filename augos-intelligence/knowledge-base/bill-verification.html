Bill Verification

Continuous comparison of utility bills against Augos-calculated estimates. Over 18 billing periods, every invoice is compared for Consumption, Demand, and Cost accuracy — transforming bill verification from a one-off finance exercise into a continuous compliance process.

1. Capability & Purpose

Municipal bills frequently contain errors. Bill Verification provides a systematic framework for detecting overcharges, missing bills, and billing variance patterns by comparing every invoice against Augos's independently calculated estimate.

  • No Date Range Selector: The only report in the platform that shows all historical periods automatically — every billing period is tracked.
  • Triple Comparison: Each period compares Bill vs Estimate for Consumption (kWh), Demand (kVA), and Cost (ZAR) simultaneously.
  • Status Indicators: ✅ Green (variance acceptable) / ❌ Red (unverified or excessive variance).
  • Gap Detection: Orange "ADD PERIOD" prompts for missing verification periods.
Catching the 100% Variance

Finance submits January's municipal bill (R926,182) and Augos calculates it should be R921,996 — a 0.5% variance (✅). But September's record shows bill=R0, estimate=R698,614 — a 100% variance (❌). Either the municipality missed the bill, or it was paid without being verified. These discrepancies are now quantified, tracked, and actionable.
