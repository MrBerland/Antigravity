The 5 Problems Energy Intelligence Solves

📄

Monthly PDF Bills

A single number with no context, no granularity, and no actionable insight. You pay it and move on.

⏰

Invisible Peak Demand

🔌

Unread Sub-Meters

You know total consumption but can't attribute it to departments, tenants, or equipment.

Reference

Glossary of Energy Terms

Term Definition
CBL Curtailment Base Load — calculated reference load for load shedding compliance
Demand Peak apparent power (kVA) measured in any 30-minute window during the billing period
Feed-in Energy exported back to the grid (e.g., from solar PV), credited against the bill
kVA Kilovolt-Ampere — apparent power (total power drawn including reactive component)
kVAr Kilovolt-Ampere Reactive — reactive power component (wasted capacity)
kW Kilowatt — real/active power (the power that does useful work)
kWh Kilowatt-hour — unit of energy consumption (what you pay for)
NERSA National Energy Regulator of South Africa — governs tariff structures
Not Measured Gap between main meter and sum of sub-meters — indicates unattributed consumption
PF Power Factor — ratio of kW to kVA. Target: ≥0.96. Below this incurs penalties.
TOU Time of Use — tariff where rates vary by period (Peak/Standard/Off-peak) and season (High/Low)
