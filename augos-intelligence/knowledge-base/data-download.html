Data Download

Pure download tool — no visualisation, no charts, no tables. Select a measurement point, choose phase handling (By Phase / Summed), date range, and format, then download bulk raw data as CSV or Excel for external analysis.

1. Capability & Purpose

Some use cases require raw data: importing into third-party energy management tools, feeding custom analytics models, or providing regulatory audit data. Data Download serves these needs with the simplest interface in the platform.

  • Phase Selection: "By Phase" delivers individual Red/White/Blue phase data; "Summed" combines them into a single series.
  • Format Options: CSV and Excel.
  • Date Range Presets: Last 7 days, Last 30 days, Last 12 months, Custom.
  • No AI-Ready File: This is a data export tool, not an analytical report — raw binary download only.
