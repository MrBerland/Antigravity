Every Lost Hour Has a Name

Standard OEE gives you A × P × Q. Augos decomposes each loss through multiple levels — separating planned from unplanned, operator-resolved from technical intervention, and active wrench from inactive wrench — until every minute is accounted for.

Flow width is proportional to time consumed. Every branch terminates in a specific, measurable, actionable variable. The deeper the decomposition, the more precise the intervention.
