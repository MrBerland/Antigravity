The Cost of Inaction

With municipal small business rates now averaging over 358.50 c/kWh, the most expensive energy is no longer the units you use — it's the units you waste because you lack visibility.

The Compounding Tariff Risk

Following the NERSA redetermination in 2026, electricity prices are compounding at three times the rate of inflation. Managing 100+ sites via delayed monthly bulk bills means identifying waste 30-45 days after the cash has left the business.

The EV & Infrastructure Shift

Dealership profiles are fundamentally changing. High-capacity DC chargers for EV lines, modern HVAC retrofits, and intense showroom lighting all stress the local grid connection. Unmanaged peak demands result in massive, punitive network capacity charges.
