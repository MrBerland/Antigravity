The Harmonic Threat

Non-linear loads (like Variable Speed Drives or LEDs) create frequency distortions called 'Harmonics' that actively sabotage standard PFC equipment, leading to capacitor burnout and catastrophic overheating.

The Augos Solution: Our hardware edge nodes track harmonic distortions (THD) in real-time. If non-linear loads shift dangerously, our cloud platform alerts engineering instantly to swap to detuned harmonic filter reactors before asset damage occurs.
